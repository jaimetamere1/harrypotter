The famous world of Harry Potter started off with the author, J.K. Rowling, writing a novel of an 11-year old orphan boy. The "debut novel" was published in 1997, and has now turned into one of the most-known franchises there are.

-- SUMMARY --

A boy named Harry Potter finds out on his eleventh birthday (by a giant banging on his door) that he is the orphaned son of two incredibly strong wizards. He is soon sent to Hogwarts, a school of Wizards and Witches, to enhance his newly-known powers, meet new friends, and more fun stuff like that. What he doesn't know, is that a very powerful bald dude with no nose, Lord Voldemort, is trying everything in his capability to kill him. Will he survive this entirely new world, or will he get avada-kedavra-d trying?
